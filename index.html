PhD Candidate in the Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences at McGill University, working with Prof. Tim Merlis. You can view my CV here.

The main goal of my research is to improve our understanding of the horizontal and vertical structure of atmospheric warming as greenhouse gas concentrations increase. I mainly use idealized models of the climate system, which by sacrificing precision, improve our understanding of the complex climate system.

I am currently searching for postdoc or research scientist positions. Do not hesitate to contact me to talk about research projects.
